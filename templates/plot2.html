Airline Delays and the effect of weather

I used a machine learning model ensemble to predict Flight DELAYs without the influence/effect of weather. For example, consider a flight from New York to San Francisco, which arrived 25 minutes late because it was raining heavily at SF. Using my model, I predict if the flight would have been delayed if it was not raining.
